Privacy Policy

How information is handled for The Friendship Practice.

Effective date

January 7, 2026

Information collected

The Friendship Practice collects information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, payment confirmation, scheduling information, intake answers, and messages you send.

How information is used

Information is used to process payment, schedule sessions, provide coaching, send session details, manage refunds, keep business records, and respond to messages.

Third-party tools

At launch, the service is expected to use Stripe for payments, Calendly or another booking tool for scheduling, Google Meet for sessions, email for communication, and a static website host.

If analytics are not active at launch, they should not be listed as active. If analytics are added later, this policy must be updated before use.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. Carole Stromboni does not directly store full credit card details.

Coaching privacy

Carole Stromboni treats coaching sessions as private. This is not therapy confidentiality, medical confidentiality, or legal privilege.

Data sharing

Carole Stromboni does not sell personal information. Information is shared only when needed to provide the service, use third-party tools, comply with law, protect legal rights, respond to safety concerns, or work with professional advisors.
